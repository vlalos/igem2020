Every project goes through the same phases. In the beginning there is the idea, which takes on more and more detailed features over time. Intensive research results in an ever clearer picture of what is possible. Additionally, it reveals potential problems and pitfalls. After this planning follows the implementation phase. It goes hand in hand with the test phase. Now it becomes apparent whether the idea can be realized successfully. However, setbacks here do not mean that the idea should be rejected. They can also be due to errors in the planning or implementation phase.
Of course also our project M.A.R.S. went through all these phases. We are proud to present our idea, the results of the different steps as well as a proposed implementation of our idea in the real world here on our Wiki.
